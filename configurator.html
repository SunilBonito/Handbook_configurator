Same parts, different products

A "bottom cabinet body" isn't a kitchen thing — it's a modular block. The same body configured for the loft becomes a storage cabinet. Different context, same validated part.

02

Constraints are enforced automatically

If a designer picks a shutter incompatible with the chosen hinge, the invalid combination is greyed out. No more silent mismatches making it to site.

03

Output is production-ready

Every valid configuration produces a complete BOM with ERP codes. Push to ODP, procurement receives the exact list, factory receives the exact build. Zero interpretation.
